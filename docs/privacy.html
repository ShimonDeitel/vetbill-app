Privacy Policy

This app is offline-first: all data stays on your device in local storage and is never transmitted anywhere. The app makes no network requests except to Apple's servers for App Store purchase processing (StoreKit), which Apple handles directly.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers in this app.

If you enable a Pro subscription or purchase, payment is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit; we never see or store your payment details.

Since all data is local to your device, uninstalling the app deletes all of your data.
